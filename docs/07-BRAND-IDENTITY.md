# Lumen: Brand Identity & Naming

## Brand Rationale
- **Lumen:** Brings clarity, transparency, and illumination to research.
- Scholarly yet modern, cross-platform and open-source.

## Tagline Ideas
- Illuminate your reviews
- Research, made brilliant
- Sift. Screen. Synthesize. Done.

## Logo & Visuals
- Combination mark: logo + type
- Colors: Research Blue (#1E40AF), Insight Gold (#F59E0B), Surface Gray (#F8FAFC)
- Clean, accessible interface

## Naming Alternatives Considered
- Siftera, Evidentia, Synthese, Meridian
- Lumen chosen for clarity and storytelling
